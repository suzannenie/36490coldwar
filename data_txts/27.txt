November 11, 1956 Department of State, Incoming Telegram No. 1270
Department of State (Confidential)
From Vienna 
To Secretary of State
No. 1270 Nov 11, 11 pm


NIACT


Sent department 1270, repeated information priority Moscow 377, Munich 22, London 36.


Reference Deptel 562 to Moscow, hier, Tormay, and Ponikiewski arrived safely Austria this evening. Leiser arrived earlier in day from Gyor, also convoy of journalists from mission Budapest.


Thompson JJL/16
